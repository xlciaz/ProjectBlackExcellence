PROJECT BLACK EXCELLENCE

    Denzel Washington

  • Born on December 28, 1954, in Mount Vernon, New York
  • Acclaimed actor known for roles in "Training Day", "Malcolm X", and "Fences"
  • Winner of two Academy Awards and a recipient of the Cecil B. DeMille Lifetime Achievement Award
  • Known for his powerful performances and contributions to Black representation in Hollywood

    Will Smith

  • Born on September 25, 1968, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania
  • Rose to fame as the star of "The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air"
  • Starred in "Men in Black", "Ali", "The Pursuit of Happyness", and "King Richard", winning an Oscar for the latter
  • One of the most bankable actors in Hollywood with a diverse filmography spanning action, drama, and comedy

    Morgan Freeman

  • Born on June 1, 1937, in Memphis, Tennessee
  • Known for his deep voice and powerful roles in "The Shawshank Redemption", "Driving Miss Daisy", and "Million Dollar Baby"
  • Academy Award winner and recipient of the AFI Life Achievement Award
  • Narrated numerous documentaries and films, becoming one of the most recognizable voices in entertainment

    Viola Davis

  • Born on August 11, 1965, in St. Matthews, South Carolina
  • First Black actress to achieve the "Triple Crown of Acting" (Oscar, Emmy, and Tony Awards)
  • Known for her performances in "Fences", "The Help", "How to Get Away with Murder", and "The Woman King"
  • A strong advocate for diversity in Hollywood and representation of Black women in leading roles

    Whoopi Goldberg

  • Born on November 13, 1955, in New York City, New York
  • One of the few entertainers to achieve EGOT status (Emmy, Grammy, Oscar, and Tony Awards)
  • Best known for her roles in "The Color Purple", "Ghost", and "Sister Act"
  • Co-host of "The View" and a strong advocate for social justice and equality

    Angela Bassett

  • Renowned for portraying strong Black female characters in "Whatâ€™s Love Got to Do with It", "Black Panther", and "The Rosa Parks Story"

    Nia Long

  • Born on October 30, 1970, in Brooklyn, New York
  • Notable for her roles in "Boyz n the Hood", "Love Jones", and "The Best Man" series
  • Award-winning actress recognized for her work in film and television
  • Has been a symbol of grace and excellence in Black Hollywood for decades

    Oprah Winfrey

  • Born on January 29, 1954, in Kosciusko, Mississippi
  • Media mogul, talk show host, and actress with an impactful role in "The Color Purple"
  • First Black woman to own her own production company and a major philanthropist
  • Recognized as one of the most influential women in the world

    Eddie Murphy

  • Born on April 3, 1961, in Brooklyn, New York
  • Comedic legend and actor known for "Coming to America", "Beverly Hills Cop", and "Shrek"
  • One of the highest-grossing actors in Hollywood
  • His versatility in both comedy and drama has earned him worldwide acclaim

    Michael B. Jordan

  • Born on February 9, 1987, in Santa Ana, California
  • Rose to fame with roles in "Fruitvale Station", "Creed", and "Black Panther"
  • Recognized as one of the most promising young Black actors in Hollywood
  • Also a producer, with a focus on telling powerful Black stories

    Bill Cosby

  • Born on July 12, 1937, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania
  • Known for "The Cosby Show", one of the most influential sitcoms in TV history
  • Previously regarded as a groundbreaking Black comedian and actor
  • Career later overshadowed by legal controversies

    Tyler Perry

  • Born on September 13, 1969, in New Orleans, Louisiana
  • Actor, playwright, and filmmaker known for creating the *Madea* franchise
  • Founder of Tyler Perry Studios, one of the largest Black-owned film studios in the world
  • Award-winning producer and advocate for Black creatives in Hollywood
