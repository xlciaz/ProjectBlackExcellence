PROJECT BLACK EXCELLENCE

What started as a humble English project, slowly began to take the shape of something more, something to fill the apparent societal void: an outlet for celebrating black excellence. Project Black Excellence (PBE) is dedicated to honoring the achievements of black individuals. Our goal is to educate, inspire, and highlight the profound impact black people have had on history and continue to have. This is a tribute to all the excellent black people there have been, there are, and there will be.
