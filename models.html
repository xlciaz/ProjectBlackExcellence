PROJECT BLACK EXCELLENCE

    Naomi Campbell

  • Born on May 22, 1970, in London, England
  • One of the original supermodels who dominated the fashion industry in the 1990s
  • First Black model to appear on the covers of French and British "Vogue"
  • Known for her striking walk, editorial work, and advocacy for diversity in fashion

    Tyra Banks

  • Born on December 4, 1973, in Inglewood, California
  • First Black woman to appear solo on the cover of "Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue"
  • Creator and host of "America's Next Top Model," shaping the modeling industry
  • Successful entrepreneur, actress, and advocate for body positivity

    Iman Mohamed Abdulmajid

  • Born on July 25, 1955, in Mogadishu, Somalia
  • One of the most influential Black models in fashion history
  • Founded "Iman Cosmetics" to cater to women of color
  • Recognized for her philanthropy and business acumen beyond modeling

    Winnie Harlow

  • Born on July 27, 1994, in Toronto, Canada
  • Known for redefining beauty standards with her vitiligo
  • Rose to fame after competing on "America's Next Top Model"
  • Has modeled for major brands like Victoria's Secret and Fendi

    Duckie Thot

  • Born on October 31, 1995, in South Sudan, raised in Australia
  • Known for her striking features and presence in high fashion
  • Walked for brands like Fenty, Balmain, and Moschino
  • A symbol of representation for dark-skinned Black models

    Adut Akech

  • Born on December 25, 1999, in South Sudan, raised in Australia
  • One of the top models of her generation, walking for Chanel, Valentino, and Versace
  • Named "Model of the Year" multiple times
  • Uses her platform to advocate for refugees and diversity in fashion

    Grace Jones

  • Born on May 19, 1948, in Jamaica
  • Model, singer, and actress known for her striking androgynous look
  • Worked with brands like Yves Saint Laurent and Kenzo
  • Also a successful musician with hits like "Pull Up to the Bumper"

    Beverly Johnson

  • Born on December 31, 1952, in Chicago, Illinois
  • First Black model to appear on the cover of "Vogue"
  • Paved the way for Black models in high fashion
  • Recognized for her impact on the industry and breaking racial barriers

    Lana Olgievie

  • Born on June 3, 1950, in Detroit, Michigan
  • Pioneering Black model in the 1970s, appearing in "Vogue" and "Essence"
  • Worked with top designers, including Halston and Stephen Burrows
  • Known for her elegance and contributions to Black representation in fashion

    Jourdan Dunn

  • Born on August 3, 1990, in London, England
  • British supermodel known for breaking barriers in the fashion industry
  • Made history as the first Black model to walk for Prada in over a decade in 2008
  • Advocates for diversity and inclusivity in the modeling world
  • Worked with top brands such as Burberry, Balmain, and Victoria's Secret
